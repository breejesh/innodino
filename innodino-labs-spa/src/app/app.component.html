About InnoDino Labs

We believe that learning to code should be as natural and joyful as playing with toys. Our mission is to introduce young minds to computational thinking through engaging, hands-on experiences that spark curiosity and build confidence.

Developed at Georgia Tech's Educational Technology program, InnoDino combines cutting-edge pedagogical research with playful, dinosaur-themed design to create an educational experience that kids love and parents trust.

Our Educational Modules

Two powerful learning experiences designed to grow with your child

🚗

DinoBot Module

Motor Robot + Ultrasonic Sensor

Watch your programs come to life! Control a motorized robot with real sensors, learning movement, conditions, and obstacle avoidance through hands-on exploration.

Learning Journey:

🟠 Basic Movement Commands
🟡 Variables & Storage
🔵 Sensor-Based Conditions
🟣 Loop Patterns & Dance
🟢 Advanced Obstacle Avoidance
Real-World Physics: Cause & effect relationships
Interactive Learning: Immediate visual feedback
Progressive Difficulty: From basics to autonomous behavior
💡

LED Matrix Module

LED Display + Ultrasonic Sensor

Create dazzling light patterns and interactive displays! Program individual LEDs to respond to sensor input, building beautiful animations and games.

Learning Journey:

🟠 Individual LED Control
🟡 Position Variables
🔵 Sensor-Responsive Displays
🟣 Pattern Generation
🟢 Interactive LED Games
Visual Creativity: Immediate artistic feedback
Mathematical Concepts: Coordinates & patterns
Game Development: Interactive entertainment programming
